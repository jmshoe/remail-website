### **FAQ Section**

### **How does the done-with-you model work?**

We build your campaigns using our advanced tech stack — but you never lose control. You get full access to every piece of the process, and we collaborate closely with you to incorporate your feedback and fine-tune the strategy. After launch, we train your team to manage the campaigns independently, or we continue working side-by-side to make sure your success keeps growing.

---

### **What does full transparency include?**

You get complete visibility into your campaigns and content, with real-time reporting at your fingertips. Our invoicing is crystal clear — no hidden fees or surprise costs. Every dollar spent and every step taken is visible so you always know exactly where you stand.

---

### **How do you ensure data-driven decisions?**

We live and breathe data. Guesswork has no place here. We rely on robust databases and detailed reports to uncover actionable insights. Every decision is backed by math, not hunches, so your campaigns are always optimized to perform.

---

### **What’s included in the simplified process?**

We automate as much as possible — from uploading your data to executing campaigns, then aggregating and reporting results. Your job? Manage the budget, set the volume, and handle the inbound leads. We take care of the rest, making direct mail marketing effortless and efficient.

---

### **How do you measure and report results?**

We track your Return On Ad Spend (ROAS) by dividing return by spend. A 3x ROAS is solid, 5x is strong, and hitting 10x means you’re absolutely crushing it. Our reporting is straightforward, so you always know how your investment is paying off.
