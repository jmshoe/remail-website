## How to Use These

1. **Browse** the sites above for inspiration
2. **Screenshot** specific sections you like
3. **Save** to the appropriate folder in `docs/design-references/`
4. **Use** `/design-from-reference <path>` to adapt the design

## Notes

- Don't copy designs verbatim - adapt principles
- Focus on layout, spacing, and typography choices
- Note what makes the design feel "premium"
- Consider how designs build trust and credibility
