# REmail Target Keyword Strategy

*Last updated: January 2026 - Based on DataForSEO search volume data*

## Executive Summary

Analysis of 80+ keywords revealed that many originally "high priority" long-tail keywords have **zero or negligible search volume**. The revised strategy focuses on:

1. **High-volume audience keywords** to capture top-of-funnel traffic
2. **List-type keywords** which have strong volume and commercial intent
3. **Competitor brand keywords** with proven search demand
4. **LOW competition gems** for quick wins
